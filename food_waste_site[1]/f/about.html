About Us

EcoEats is a food waste management system designed to reduce the food wastage and use it in a useful way. This system also provide a platform to people where they can donate food. We are in contact with 150+ banquets. We collect the donated food and distribute it among the people who really need it. It not only reduces food waste but also hunger, which is a major problem of the world. We believe nutritious food should go to people, not landfills. This website is developed by:
